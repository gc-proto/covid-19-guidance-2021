Use this service to register your positive result after using a home rapid test. Isolate yourself or your child from others to prevent spreading COVID-19 to others.

Report your positive test result:

  • to have your result recorded in your area's COVID-19 data
  • so that you have a record of the result

Report one positive result per person, as soon as possible after taking the test.

Testing an idea: This form is not yet collecting and saving your input - it is presented as an idea to assist Canadians.
