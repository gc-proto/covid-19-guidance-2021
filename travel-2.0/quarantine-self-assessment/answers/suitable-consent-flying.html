Assess your quarantine plan - Suitable with consent

You selected that you:

  • have access to basic necessities without leaving your place of quarantine
  • won't be in a group living environment
  • are sharing your place of quarantine with someone in an at risk group
  • have a separate bedroom and bathroom
  • entering by air

Based on the information you provided, the person you're sharing your place of quarantine with must consent to you staying with them.

Complete a consent form at the time of entry: A government representative at the port of entry will request information from you to complete a form confirming the consent and giving authorization to proceed. This form cannot be completed in advance. To complete the assessment at the border, a government representative will call the person to obtain their consent. You will need to provide their:

  • legal name
  • date of birth
  • full address
  • phone number

If the person is your parent or a minor in a parent-minor relationship with you, they must agree to quarantine with you as well as part of the consent form process.

Throughout your quarantine:

  • avoid contact with others
  • stay in separate rooms
  • use a separate bathroom
  • keep surfaces clean
  • avoid sharing personal items
  • limit interactions with others in the household

Mandatory hotel stay: On arrival to Canada, you will be required to take a COVID-19 test before you leave the airport. Following the test, you will await the results in your reserved stop over hotel. If you receive a negative result on your arrival test, you may be able to move on to your place of quarantine.
