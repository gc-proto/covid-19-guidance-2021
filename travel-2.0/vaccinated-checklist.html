Checklists for fully vaccinated travellers

These checklist are for fully vaccinated travellers to Canada. Entry, testing and quarantine requirements vary by length of trip for Canadians, and whether you've been outside of Canada and the U.S. within the last 14 days.

Check if you qualify as a fully vaccinated traveller

If you've been in countries with temporary COVID-19 Omicron entry restrictions, don't use these checklists. Follow the temporary enhanced requirements.
