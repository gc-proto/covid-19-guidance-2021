You indicated that you are:

  • a foreign national

Have you received at least 2 COVID-19 vaccine doses or a combination of at least 2 doses of:

  • AstraZeneca (Vaxzevria, COVISHIELD, ChAdOx1-S,AZD1222)
  • Bharat Biotech (Covaxin, BBV152 A, B, C)
  • Janssen/Johnson & Johnson (Ad26.COV2.S) - 1 dose
  • Moderna (Spikevax, mRNA-1273)
  • Pfizer-BioNTech (Comirnaty, tozinameran, BNT162b2)
  • Sinopharm (Beijing) BBIBP-CorV (Vero Cells)
  • Sinovac (CoronaVac, PiCoVacc)
  • a combination of at least 2 doses of the above vaccines
  • none of the above (other vaccines, unvaccinated, single dose, recovered)
Accepted vaccines

Vaccines accepted by the Government of Canada for the purpose of travel to and within Canada:

  • AstraZeneca (Vaxzevria, COVISHIELD, ChAdOx1-S, AZD1222)
  • Bharat Biotech (Covaxin, BBV152 A, B, C)
  • Janssen/Johnson & Johnson (Ad26.COV2.S)
  • Moderna (Spikevax, mRNA-1273)
  • Pfizer-BioNTech (Comirnaty, tozinameran, BNT162b2)
  • Sinopharm (Beijing) BBIBP-CorV (Vero Cells)
  • Sinovac (CoronaVac, PiCoVacc)

To qualify as a fully vaccinated traveller, you must have received at least 10 full days prior to the day you travel:

  • at least 2 doses of a vaccine accepted by the Government of Canada for the purpose of travel
  • or a mix of 2 accepted vaccines
  • or at least 1 dose of the Janssen/Johnson & Johnson vaccine

Unvaccinated children and dependents

Unvaccinated children, youth and adult dependents may accompany a parent, step-parent, guardian or tutor who qualifies as a fully vaccinated traveller when they enter Canada.
