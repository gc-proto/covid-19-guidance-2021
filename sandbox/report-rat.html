Use this service to register your result after using a rapid test to check if you're infectious with COVID-19.

A rapid test is a test you do yourself or for your child. It shows your results to you as a line or lines on a stick or other device.

Report your test result

  • to have your result recorded in your area's data
  • so that you have a record of the result

Report one result at a time, as soon as possible after taking the test

Answer a few questions to find out if you may be allowed to enter Canada.

What was the result of your test?

  • Positive
  • Negative
  • Unclear - I couldn't tell
