You selected that you:

  • have a place to quarantine for 14 days or longer
  • have access to basic necessities without leaving your place of quarantine
  • are sharing your place and can avoid all contact
  • aren't sharing your place with someone at risk of more severe disease or outcomes

Based on the information you provided, your place of quarantine is likely considered suitable. Final determination will be made at the border crossing by a Government of Canada representative.

During your quarantine, you must avoid all contact with anyone who isn't also quarantining.

  • Ensure you are not in the same room with them at any time

You must also:

  • not have any visits from family or guests, indoors or outdoors
  • have a phone or access to email so government representatives can reach you
  • stay in your home, hotel room, unit or apartment. Do not use shared spaces, including:
    • lobbies
    • restaurants or dining areas
    • gyms or pools
    • communal hallways
  • only leave your place of quarantine to seek essential or emergency medical services (use private transportation) and:
    • if you must interact with anyone, wear a well-fitting recommended mask
    • make sure interactions are as few and as brief as possible
    • keep the greatest distance possible away from others
