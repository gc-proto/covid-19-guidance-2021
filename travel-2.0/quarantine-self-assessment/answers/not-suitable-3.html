Assess your quarantine plan - Not suitable

You selected that you:

  • won't be in a group living environment
  • have access to basic necessities without leaving your place of quarantine
  • are sharing your place of quarantine
  • do not have a separate bedroom or bathroom

Based on the information you provided, your place of quarantine is not suitable.

You cannot quarantine in a setting where you cannot separate yourself from others. You need to have access to living quarters (bathroom, bedroom) that are separate from people you did not travel with.

What you need to do

Make alternate arrangements

You won't likely be allowed to quarantine at this location. Please make alternate arrangements within your means. This could include a hotel or other suitable location.

Make your quarantine plan

If a government representative at the port of entry determines that your quarantine plan is not suitable, you will be required to stay in a PHAC approved facility for the 14-day mandatory quarantine period at your expense.
