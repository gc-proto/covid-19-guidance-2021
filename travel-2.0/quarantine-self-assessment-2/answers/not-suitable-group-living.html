Based on the information you provided, your place of quarantine is not suitable.

You cannot quarantine in group living environments. This means a location where a number of people reside in close proximity for either a limited or an extended period of time. In most cases, residents share common rooms such as a kitchen or bathroom, they may or may not have private sleeping quarters, and they may share household items that can become at risk for contamination.

Make alternate arrangements

You won't be allowed to quarantine at this location. You should make alternate arrangements within your means. This could include a hotel or other suitable location.
